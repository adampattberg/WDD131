Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to inform users of which type of RC car is the best option for them.

Audience

My audience are those who are new to RC cars and would consider themselves beginners. These are generally individuals willing to spend a lower amount of money than hobbiests.

Dynamic elements

The JavaScript for my site would be utilized for showing the user different colorways of a specific RC car model.

Pseudocode for your project

I first plan to code the homepage with the process of top to bottom. This means starting with the header and the nav, then moving to the body and its elements, finishing with the footer. I will then go on to do this same process on the child pages. Once this is complete, I can begin with the CSS of the site, again beginning at the homepage going top to bottom. After the CSS is complete, I can then add any special elements I want with JS, making my site unique and interactive with users.

Wireframes

I created a wireframe for the home page and the two child pages. The child pages are identical to each other.

Landing page

The home page will describe the hobby of RC cars and assist the user on which vehicle is the right decision for them based on their goals and use case.

Page 2 and 3

Page two and three shows the RC cars themselves and gives details about the vehicle and its usability.
