Traxxas Slash 2WD XL-5

The Traxxas Slash is the pinnacle of beginner RC cars. It is designed to be driven hard in every condition. It is overbuilt from the factory and has a large community that supports every modification you can imagine. The community and aftermarket support is the cornerstone to the Slashs' success. You are able to get every question answered and find any part you need, whether you are repairing or upgrading the component. From the factory it comes with a brushed electric motor, 8.4V Ni-MH 3000 mAh battery, and USB-C fast charging. The only item you need to begin driving are the batteries for the remote controller.
